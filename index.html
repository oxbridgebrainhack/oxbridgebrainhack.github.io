About

The Oxbridge Brainhack 2019 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2019, jointly organized by researchers from the University of Cambridge and University of Oxford. The brainhack is a student-run hackathon and unconference that brings together researchers and technologists from a myriad of disciplines to work together on innovative neuroscience projects. We will bring together neuroscience researchers from different departments across the two universities to celebrate interdisciplinary, collaborative and open science.
